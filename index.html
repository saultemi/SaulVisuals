Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your session.

How do I book a session?

+

Simply fill out the contact form or message me directly. I'll respond within 24–48 hours to confirm your date, discuss your vision, and send a booking agreement. A deposit is required to hold your date.

How far in advance should I book?

+

I recommend booking 3–4 weeks ahead for portrait sessions, and 2–3 months for quinceañeras. Newborn sessions are best booked during your second trimester — we'll schedule near your due date and confirm once baby arrives.

Where will our session take place?

+

Sessions can happen at a location of your choice — parks, urban spots, your home, a studio, or any meaningful place. I'll help you pick somewhere that works with the light and your style. Travel fees may apply beyond 30 miles.

When will I receive my photos?

+

Most sessions are delivered within 1–2 weeks via a private online gallery. Newborn sessions may take up to 2 weeks. Rush delivery is available as an add-on.

What should we wear?

+

I'll send a personalized style guide after booking. Coordinating tones (not exact matches) look great for families. Avoid busy patterns and logos. Most importantly — wear something that makes you feel confident.

What if it rains or we need to reschedule?

+

Weather happens! If we need to reschedule due to weather, I'll work with you to find a new date at no extra charge. I ask for at least 48 hours notice for non-weather reschedules.

Do you shoot quinceañera events AND portraits?

+

Yes! I offer both formal portrait sessions (pre-event) and full event coverage. Many clients book both. Reach out and we'll build the perfect package for your special day.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Absolutely. A 50% deposit secures your date, with the remaining balance due the day of your session. For larger packages, custom plans can be arranged — just ask!
