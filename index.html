Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session?

+

Simply fill out the contact form below or send me a message directly. I'll reach out within 24–48 hours to confirm your date, discuss your vision, and send over a booking agreement. A deposit is required to hold your date.

How far in advance should I book?

+

I recommend booking at least 3–4 weeks in advance for portrait sessions, and 2–3 months for quinceañeras. Newborn sessions are best booked during your second trimester to secure a spot close to your due date — we schedule and then confirm once baby arrives.

Where will our session take place?

+

Sessions can take place at a location of your choice — parks, urban areas, your home, a studio, or any meaningful spot. I'll help you choose a location that complements your style and works with the available light. Travel fees may apply beyond 30 miles.

When will I receive my photos?

+

Most sessions are delivered within 1–2 weeks via a private online gallery where you can view, download, and share your images. Newborn sessions may take up to 2 weeks. Rush delivery (48–72 hrs) is available as an add-on.

What should we wear?

+

I'll send a personalized style guide after booking. Generally, coordinating tones (not matching exactly) look great for families. Avoid busy patterns and logos. Most importantly — wear something that makes you feel confident and like yourself. I'm happy to advise!

What if it rains or we need to reschedule?

+

Weather happens! If we need to reschedule due to weather or unforeseen circumstances, I'll work with you to find a new date at no extra charge. I ask for at least 48 hours notice for non-weather-related reschedules so I can offer that spot to another family.

Do you shoot quinceañera events AND portraits?

+

Yes! I offer both formal quinceañera portrait sessions (pre-event) and full event coverage. Many clients book a portrait session beforehand and also have me present at the celebration. Reach out and let's build the perfect package for your special day.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Absolutely. A 50% deposit is required to secure your date, with the remaining balance due on the day of the session. For larger packages (quinceañeras, extended events), custom payment plans can be arranged. Just ask!
